Planet of the Kyvera

Planet Kenaria is a super-massive Earthy planet with a low density and a long day-night cycle of 148 hours. Compared to Earth, the planet has a lower ocean to land ratio and much of the surface of the planet varies extremely in elevation.


Kenaria is home to a wide variety of extreme environments. With its mix of long day-night cycles, extreme verticality, and unique weather events the planet hosts several unique biomes the kyvera have adapted to.


For example, the sandvera subspecies is home to the liquid sand biome, which is a large, completely oceanic biome entirely comprised of liquid-like sand and small islands.
