Katari Kityama

Katari Kityama here, I'm the creator of the kyvera species. In 2022 during my high school years, I created the kyvera over biology classwork with a simple sketch. My friend chose the color folly pink and that is how the species started.


The original sketch was meant to look like a chinchilla, but it looked nothing like that which is probably for the best. Modern kyvera look nothing like rodents and are some mix between mammals and reptiles.


I drew the species quite frequently and due to my quickly improving art skills among other factors I gained popularity and started growing a community around the species.


Unfortunately, I have since let the project die despite still occasionally drawing my beloved kyvera. You can view some of these drawings on the right.
